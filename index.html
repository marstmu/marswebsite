About Us

The Metropolitan Aerospace Rocket Society (MARS) opens the doors to designing, manufacturing, and launching model rockets. The team, composed of passionate, energetic, and eager students, hosts tutorials to teach students all they need to know to create their own rockets, and they provide support throughout the process.

During the summer, MARS invites the top teams of the semesters to attend the Launch Canada Conference to attain their L1 HPR certifications.

This year, MARS has had over 250 students interested in entering the world of model rocketry and has 500 members in our Discord.
