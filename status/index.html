    Your status

    By now your team should have a decent CAD of your rocket. You should have used the BOM from the Rocket Guide. Half of the teams have done the PDR, which is meant to showcase your current progress. This is not a requirement but you loose 15% on the marking if you opt out of it. Other than that optional thing, there is nothing teams need to do right now.

    As always feel free to reach out in your team specific discord channel if you are feeling confused about anything!
